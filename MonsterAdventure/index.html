Monster Adventure is a gripping, turn-based RPG with roots in genre-defining PC classics like Baldurâ€™s Gate and Zork. Monster Adventure combines the thrill of loot collection with intense, heart-pounding turn-based combat. Players take on the role of Amp R. Sand, a rogue dungeon crawler bent on achieving glory in the Arena, an interdimensional plane accessible only via a strange portal that appeared mysteriously in the small village of Astrix he calls home. Amp must gear up and set out to face vicious beasts and do whatever it takes to be victorious.

In the alpha version of the game, players will face three challenging foes, which appear at random each time they brave the portal. It would be wise to first explore the town and collect any loot Amp encounters. Once players are ready, they must simply reach the southern border of the town to enter the portal and summon a beast to the Arena. Once the beast has been slain, Amp will return to his town of Astrix to collect additional healing potions before taking on the next towering foe.
